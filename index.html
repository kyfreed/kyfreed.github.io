Family-friendly entertainment.

Bend may be famous for its beer, but that's not what we're about. We want to promote a gameplay environment that children and adults can enjoy. That's why we placed our store away from any bars as well as not serving alcohol ourselves, instead opting for alternative snack items such as nachos and smoothies.

Many ways to play!

This isn't just a game store where you browse for a bit, maybe buy a game, and leave. We have several services that will make the Cascade Game Lodge one of your favorite places to spend time with your friends.

Rent in!

We know that board games are expensive. That's why we don't expect you to buy every game you want to play! For a fixed fee per game, you can play a session with any of the games we have in our library.

Rent out!

Not convenient for all of your friends to meet up at this location? That's fine! We allow all of our games to be checked out for a short time so you can get together with friends in the comfort of your own home!

Bring your own!

You aren't limited to what's available in store! For a small fee, you can use our table space to play your own games!
