Hi. I'm Kyle.

I'm an avid fan of tabletop games; board games in particular. Card games come in a close second. I grew up playing a lot of Uno, and I've collected way too many playing cards at this point. I regret that I haven't played D&D very often at all -- scheduling issues make it really hard for everyone I guess. If you're playing and you think I would be interested, give me a call :)

Modern Games

So why did I start the Cascade Game Lodge anyway?

I simply feel like it fulfills a need. There is only one other business like this one in Bend, and that is Modern Games.
However, I have always had one problem with this store: it heavily leans on the culture of enjoying alcoholic beverages while hanging out with friends. I understand this business strategy, and it has its place; but I wanted to tackle this problem a little differently, focusing on giving families an enjoyable experience that is both child and adult-friendly.
